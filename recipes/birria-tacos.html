Beef birria tacos

Home made Beef Birria Tacos with consome

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck roast
  • Dried chile peppers
  • Seasonings
  • Tomatoes
  • Tortillas
  • Onion and Cilantro

Steps

  1. Prepare dried peppers
  2. Season the beef
  3. Grill the tomatoes
  4. Combine all in a large pot
  5. Cook for 4 hours
  6. Remove beef from pot and shred
  7. Partially fry the tortillas
  8. Make the tacos and add cheese if desired
