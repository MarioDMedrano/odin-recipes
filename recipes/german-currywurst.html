German Currywurst

Pub style German Currywurst

Ingredients

  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Kiebalsa
  • Curry Powder
  • Chili sauce
  • Onion salt

Steps

  1. Prepare oven
  2. Add canned tomatoes to saucepan
  3. Grill kiebalsa sausage until cooked
  4. Add tomato sauce over sausage then add onion salt and curry powder
  5. Serve with favorite German beer
